What is HDI? HDI: Human Development Index Definition: a summary measure of average achievement in key dimensions of human development: a long and healthy life, being knowledgeable and have a decent standard of living. Sustained by four main pillars: equity, sustainability, productivity, empowerment. Rated on a scale of 0-1, 0 being lowest HDI and 1 being highest HDI How it intersects with global suicide rates: intuitively, we would assume the lower the HDI, the higher the suicide rates- a negative correlation. The data surprised and showed a strong positive correlation. The higher the HDI, the higher number of global suicide. This leads us to believe that higher percieved quality of life contributes to people wanting to commit suicide.
