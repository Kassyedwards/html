Global Suicide Rates

The Project

We wanted to use data from two independent datasets pulled from Kaggle. One dataset included information about global suicide rates since the mid 80s for men and women across the world. The other dataset provided relevant information about geography, GDP, and other factors that we merged in order to make higher level analysis and inferences about suicide causality.

After cleaning and assembling the data we wanted to look for underlying trends and causal relationships using matplotlib. We analyzed the global suicide rate over time, a comparison of age groups, and regions, and finally completed an analysis on the HDI and rate of global suicide.
