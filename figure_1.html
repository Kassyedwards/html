Here we wanted to look for global trends and explore suicide rates over the entire length of the data set. We found that in 1995 global suicide rates peaked and have slowly been declining ever since. The peak in 1995 can be attributed to the recent fall of the Soviet Union and then the drop of suicide rates after 1995 can be attributed to the stigma on suicide beginning fade as well is more of a cultural focus on suicide prevention. The minor spike after 2005 can also be attributed to the rise of social media and advent of facebook.
